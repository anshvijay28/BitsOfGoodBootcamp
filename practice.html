When wildfires forced the people of my city to evacuate from their homes, I decided that was the perfect time to begin learning python. Although an unfortunate event, the wildfire did give me a lot of time to do an online python course. I had already been interested in learning python for a while, but I never had the patience to sit through the hours of learning variables, if statements, loops, and other rudimentary programming structures. This course is what ignited my passion for computer science.
